About Me

I am a sophomore at SUNY Purchase College studying Graphic Design with a minor in Art History.
  I was originally a Photography major until I found I liked Graphic Design more. 
 I am proficient in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, and Lightroom. 
 I enjoy creating layouts, mixing type with images, working in Illustrator, creating color based pieces, and editing images. 
 I am interested in how colors interact and play with each other.


Fun Facts

My elbow hyper-extends which makes archery annoying.
I love baking and experimenting with new recipes. My current favorite things to make are French macaroons.
When I’m bored, I make very meticulous cuttings out of paper or vinyl while watching Netflix.
My favorite food is pie.
